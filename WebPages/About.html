Farhan's Islamic Bookstore



Our Mission:

To provide a space where those with the zeal for knowledge can come and purchase the books to further themselves in their studies. We aim to bring you the widest array of Islamic books to diversify your learning experience at an affordable price. May Allah grant you steadfastness in your studies. Ameen



Our History:

Established in 1999. Our first collection was Quran, Quran Commentary and Hadith books. With the tawfiq of Allah we managed to increase our collection within the next two years to bring about Islamic history, architect, economics and more. We have scholars who are well versed in all of the books that are in our collection and have been helping our customers since the beginning the store started. We look forward to your visit.
